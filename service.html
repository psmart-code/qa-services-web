How We Work

Clear steps, transparent communication, and artifacts you can share with your stakeholders.

1) Kickoff

We align on scope, environments, deadlines, and success criteria.

2) Test Plan

Coverage matrix, priorities, devices/browsers, and test data strategy.

3) Execution

Test cases + exploratory sessions; bugs documented with repro steps, evidence, and severity.

4) Reporting

Executive summary, metrics, risk assessment, and recommendations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I pay with Takenos?

Yes. We also accept Stripe, PayPal, and US wire transfers.

When can you start?

Typically within 48–72 hours after kickoff and environment access.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes. We can sign your NDA or provide ours.

How do you report bugs?

We use your tools (Jira, Trello, Azure DevOps, Linear) or provide a shared board. Reports include steps, expected vs actual, environment, and evidence (screenshots/video).
